Business Intelligence Analyst

We are looking for a Business Intelligence Analyst to join our operations and reporting team. The role focuses on transforming business data into clear dashboards, reports, and practical insights that help managers make better decisions.

The successful candidate will work closely with operations, finance, and customer service teams to understand reporting needs, clean and validate datasets, build dashboards, and explain trends to non-technical stakeholders.

Essential requirements:

- Strong SQL skills and experience working with relational databases
- Advanced Excel skills, including formulas, pivot tables, and data cleaning
- Experience building dashboards in Power BI or Tableau
- Ability to clean, validate, and prepare business datasets
- Strong data visualisation and reporting skills
- Ability to gather requirements from business stakeholders
- Clear written and verbal communication skills
- At least 2 years of experience in business intelligence, data analysis, reporting, or business analysis

Desirable requirements:

- Experience with Python for data analysis
- Understanding of data quality frameworks and validation rules
- Experience documenting reporting processes and data definitions
- Experience in financial services, healthcare, retail, or operations reporting
- Basic understanding of predictive analytics or machine learning
- Interest in responsible AI and ethical use of data

Education:

A bachelor degree in information systems, business analytics, commerce, data analytics, computer science, or a related field is preferred, but equivalent professional experience will also be considered.

Key responsibilities:

- Develop and maintain dashboards for operational and management reporting
- Use SQL to extract, join, and validate data from internal systems
- Clean and prepare datasets for analysis and reporting
- Work with stakeholders to define reporting requirements and success measures
- Translate business questions into data analysis tasks
- Present findings using clear visualisations and plain-English explanations
- Document dashboard logic, data sources, and reporting definitions
- Identify data quality issues and recommend process improvements
- Support continuous improvement of reporting workflows
